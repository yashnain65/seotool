How to submit your sitemap to Google:

  1. Generate and download your sitemap.xml file
  2. Upload it to your website's root directory (public_html/)
  3. Go to Google Search Console
  4. Select your property and click on "Sitemaps"
  5. Enter your sitemap URL (e.g., https://yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml)
  6. Click "Submit"
